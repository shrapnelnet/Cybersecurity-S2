Malware

  • Malware

    What is Malware

    Malware is piece of code created with the intention of causing negative effects for the user. This can be cause harmful reults such as:

    • devices becoming unusable
    • comprimised data
    • stolen credentials
    • using or buying products/services on your behalf
    • unauthorized access to content of the infected device

    Types of Malware:

  • Types

    Viruses

    A virus is a type of software created to infect legitmate software with itself and using that to replicate itself across various network and onto other files if it is allowed to activate. due to the abilty to easily spead an unchecked virus can massivly harm any device it gets onto as it could risk replicating onto other files on the device makingit even harder to remove.

  • Types

    Trojans

    Trojans masquerade as legitimate software to trick users into downloading malicious software, which can be used to:

    • Capture data
    • Gain unauthorized access to networks
    • Delete, modify, or capture data
    • Harvest a device as part of a botnet
    • Spy on a device

    Typically spread through social engineering tactics, such as phishing, trojans can pose a serious threat to your security. The payload of a trojan usually consists of a backdoor that provides the attacker with unauthorized access to the infected computer. This can potentially grant access to personal information, including:

    • Internet activity
    • Banking login credentials
    • Passwords
    • Personally Identifiable Information (PII)

    Trojans can also be used to install other malware, such as ransomware, on your device.
